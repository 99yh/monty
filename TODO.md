## Mandatory tasks

Implement the following opcodes:

    0. `push` <int> and `pall`
    1. `pint`
    2. `pop`
    3. `swap`
    4. `add`
    5. `nop` nothing!

## Advanced tasks

Implement the following opcodes:

    6. `sub`
    7. `div`
    8. `mul`
    9. `mod`
    10. `#` comments
    11. `pchr`
    12. `pstr`
    13. `rotl`
    14. `rotr`
    15. `stack` and `queue`
